J-HotelReservation is the way to automate reservation on your site.Enable bookings online and automate the reservation process to increase the efficiency of your overall lodging facility management.
The key features are:
  • 4 easy reservation steps, from booking dates to final confirmation.
  • multiple payment options
  • flexibility in managing reservations
  • manage rooms, taxes, extra optiona, email templates, reviews
  • simple, flexible way to define and assign room options
  • Easy to manage from the backend
  • create multiple offers
  • airport transfer
  • simple, flexible way to define room prices
  • and many more...
Important Please refer to the J-HotelReservation User Manual for a complete guide on how to setup the reservation component. The User Manual is available for download along with the purchase. We will guide through the neccesary steps to make this work.
