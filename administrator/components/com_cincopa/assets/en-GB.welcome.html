Quickstart

While editing your content, use the new Cincopa Media button at the bottom of the editor window for inserting media. A wizard will popup and will guide you step by step through the creation process and will even embed the gallery for you.

For more information - especially before using the advanced features or the pro account , please visit our http://help.cincopa.com.
You can invoke the support site at any time after clicking on the Cincopa Media button and clicking the Help button.
The support site also provides a collection of useful examples.

Good Luck!
